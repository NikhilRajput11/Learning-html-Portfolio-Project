The Best Movies According to Nikhil

My top 3 movies of 2023.

  • 12th Fail

    • This is my favourite bollywood movie of 2023. I love the inspirational story.
    It is an Indian Hindi-language biographical drama film produced, written and directed by Vidhu Vinod Chopra.
    Starring: Vikrant Massey, Medha Shankar, Anant Joshi, Anshumaan Pushkar
    OTT: Hotstar

  • Bholaa

    • It is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Ajay Devgn.
    Starring: Ajay Devgn, Tabu, Abhishek Bachchan, Sanjay Mishra, Gajraj Rao.
    OTT: Amazon Prime

  • Kathal

    • Kathal - A Jackfruit Mystery also known as Kathal, is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language satirical comedy drama film written by Ashok Mishra and directed by Yashowardhan Mishra.
    Starring: Sanya Malhotra, Vijay Raaz, Rajpal Yadav.
    Super beautiful film in comedy genre.
    OTT: Netflix
